What is a technology and its features?


Technology is defined as a modern invention and is also defined as the activity of creating and influencing technological results in the world. The purpose of technological results to improve people's capacities and extend human potential. They affect the social and natural words by altering the created world in good and/or harmful ways. Technology is both a consumer and a producer of technological information. When technological knowledge is provided to help the effective development of technological outputs, technological communities accept it as valid. All technological has a historical context in which it is impacted and influenced by social and culture. Technological practise is becoming more multidisciplinary, depending on collaboration between technologies and individuals form other disciplines more than ever before.
